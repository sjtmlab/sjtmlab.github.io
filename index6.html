About the Maker Lab

What We Do

The St. James Town Maker Lab is a community space where everyone can learn, build, and create together. We provide access to tools, workshops, and mentorship in areas like coding, electronics, cyber security, content creation, and computer repair. Our goal is to empower the community to explore technology and develop hands-on skills in a collaborative environment.
