St. Jamestown Maker Lab

Build. Learn. Create. Level Up.

About Us

St. Jamestown Maker Lab is a free community space for youth (13–18) to explore technology, creativity, and real-world skills. We focus on hands-on learning, digital skills, and helping young people turn ideas into reality.

What You Can Do

💻 Coding & Tech

Learn programming, build apps, and understand how computers really work.

🔌 Electronics

Create projects with LEDs, sensors, and microcontrollers.

🌐 Networking & Cybersecurity

Learn how the Internet works and how to stay secure online.

🎥 Content Creation

Create videos, stream, and learn editing for YouTube and social media.

🔧 Repair & Build

Fix computers, upgrade hardware, and build systems from scratch.

🚀 Projects

Work on your own ideas or join group builds and challenges.

Tools & Skills

We use real tools and real skills: coding, computer repair, networking, cybersecurity basics, video editing, and creative technology.
